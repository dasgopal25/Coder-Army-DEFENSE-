Weekly Structure

    5 workout days + 2 rest or light activity days

    Alternate between strength, cardio, and mobility-focused days

    Combine compound exercises for fat burning and muscle building

  • Workout Plan

  • Day 1 – Full Body Strength

    • Warm-up – 5 min brisk walk or cycling
    • Squats – 3 sets × 12 reps
    • Push-ups – 3 sets × 10–15 reps
    • Bent-over rows (dumbbells/barbell) – 3 sets × 12 reps
    • Plank – 3 sets × 30 sec
    • Cool down stretching – 5 min

  • Day 2 – Cardio & Core

    • Warm-up – 5 min light jog
    • HIIT (High-Intensity Interval Training)
    • 30 sec sprint, 60 sec walk × 8 rounds
    • Mountain climbers – 3 sets × 20
    • Leg raises – 3 sets × 15
    • Russian twists – 3 sets × 20
    • Cool down stretch – 5 min

  • Day 3 – Lower Body & Endurance

    • Warm-up – 5 min jump rope
    • Lunges – 3 sets × 12 per leg
    • Deadlifts (light to moderate weight) – 3 sets × 10
    • Calf raises – 3 sets × 20
    • Side plank – 2 sets × 20 sec per side
    • Cool down stretch – 5 min

  • Day 4 – Active Recovery / Mobility

  • 20 min light walk or swimming

  • Yoga poses:

    1. Downward dog – 3 × 30 sec
    2. Cobra stretch – 3 × 20 sec
    3. Child’s pose – 3 × 30 sec
  • Deep breathing exercises – 5 min

  • Day 5 – Upper Body & Power

    • Warm-up – 5 min rowing or cycling
    • Dumbbell shoulder press – 3 sets × 12
    • Pull-ups (or assisted) – 3 sets × 8
    • Dumbbell bicep curls – 3 sets × 15
    • Tricep dips – 3 sets × 12
    • Plank with shoulder taps – 3 sets × 20 taps
    • Cool down stretch – 5 min

  • Day 6 – Optional Light Activity / Rest

    • Light walk, stretching, or meditation
    • Avoid heavy lifting

  • Day 7 – Rest

    • Complete rest or stretching


  • Diet Guidelines

    Eat every 3–4 hours

    Stay hydrated – 2.5–3 liters of water per day

    Avoid fried foods, refined sugar, and processed snacks

    Include lean proteins, whole grains, healthy fats, and plenty of vegetables

    1. Breakfast

      • Oatmeal with chia seeds, banana slices, and almonds
      • 1 boiled egg or paneer

    2. Mid-Morning

      • Greek yogurt or buttermilk with cucumber
      • Handful of mixed nuts

    3. Lunch

      • Grilled chicken breast / fish / tofu
      • Brown rice or quinoa
      • Mixed vegetables sautéed with olive oil

    4. Evening Snack

      • Fruit smoothie with spinach, berries, and protein powder
      • Roasted chickpeas

    5. Dinner

      • Lentil soup or chicken stew
      • Whole wheat chapati
      • Steamed vegetables and salad

    6. Before bed (optional)

      • Warm milk with turmeric or a protein shake
